I am a Master’s student in **Computer Technology** at the School of Computer Science and Technology, **Xi’an Jiaotong University (XJTU)** (2024–present), after completing my Bachelor’s degree in **Software Engineering** at the College of Information Science and Engineering, **Hunan University (HNU)** (2020–2024).

I am currently participating in a joint industry-academia training program with **teleAI**, under the co-supervision of:
- **Academic Advisor**: [Prof. Bo Li](https://gr.xjtu.edu.cn/web/boblee) (XJTU)  
- **Industry Advisor**: [Dr. Shuangyong Song](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=cFHBCNEAAAAJ&hl=en) (China Telecom / teleAI)

My research interests include **Large Language Models (LLMs)**, **Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG)**, **Agentic RAG**, **Table Reasoning**, and **Time Series Forecasting**.
